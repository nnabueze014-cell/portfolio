Project 3 – AI Lead Qualification & Cold Outreach Automation

This project demonstrates how outbound sales workflows can be automated using lead intelligence tools, email outreach platforms, and AI-powered reply classification.

The system was designed to simulate how modern sales teams automate outbound prospecting while reducing the manual effort required to review and qualify incoming email replies.

Prospects are sourced using a lead intelligence platform, imported into a cold email campaign, and engaged through a structured outreach sequence. When replies are received, automation workflows analyze the message using AI and assign lead qualification scores that determine the next action.

System Architecture

The system consists of three operational layers working together:

  • Lead Intelligence Layer – Prospect discovery and filtering
  • Outreach Layer – Cold email campaign management
  • Automation & AI Layer – Reply detection, AI scoring, and workflow routing

Lead Intelligence – Prospect Discovery

Prospects were sourced using a lead intelligence platform where companies were filtered based on industry, company size, and operational roles relevant to service-based organizations.

Prospect Dataset Preparation

Qualified prospects were exported and prepared for outreach campaigns. Additional operational fields were added to support automation workflows such as lead scoring, engagement status, and escalation flags.

Cold Outreach Campaign (Lemlist)

A structured outbound email campaign was built using Lemlist. The campaign consisted of a three-step sequence designed to initiate conversations with operational decision-makers.

  • Initial outreach email
  • Follow-up message
  • Breakup email

Email Reply Detection

When a prospect replies to the outreach email, Lemlist triggers automation workflows through Zapier. These workflows capture the reply content and send it to an AI classifier for analysis.

AI Lead Qualification

Incoming replies are analyzed using AI to determine the intent and qualification level of the lead.

  • Positive interest
  • Neutral / information request
  • Negative response

The AI assigns a lead score which is then used to determine follow-up actions such as scheduling meetings, sending additional information, or removing the contact from future campaigns.

Automation Outcome

  • Automated reply classification
  • Reduced manual lead triaging
  • Faster identification of qualified prospects
  • Structured outbound lead management workflow
