Project 2 – Front-Desk to Support Operations with Automated SLA Escalations

Project Context:
This project explores how technical service companies manage customer requests from initial contact through operational resolution. The goal was to design an end-to-end system that mirrors how service organizations handle support tickets, operational task execution, and SLA monitoring.

System Architecture:

  1. Customer Support Layer
    Customers submit requests through a support portal which automatically creates structured tickets with priority levels and SLA timers.
  2. Automation Layer
    Ticket events trigger automated workflows that process ticket data and generate operational tasks.
  3. Operations Execution Layer
    Tasks are routed to engineers within a structured project management pipeline where progress and SLA timelines are tracked.

Key Outcomes:

  • Full visibility from customer request to resolution
  • Automated handoff between support and operations
  • SLA monitoring and escalation triggers
  • Improved accountability for operational tasks

Concepts Applied: Service Desk Operations • SLA Monitoring • Workflow Automation • Escalation Logic • Cross-Team Visibility

Project 3 – AI Lead Qualification & Cold Outreach Automation

Project Context:
Outbound prospecting is widely used in modern sales operations, but reviewing every reply manually can slow down the lead qualification process. This project explores how automation and AI can assist in identifying promising leads faster and routing them into structured follow-up workflows.

System Workflow:

  1. Prospects are sourced and filtered using Apollo.io
  2. Qualified contacts are imported into a Lemlist outreach campaign
  3. A structured cold email sequence is sent to prospects
  4. Email replies trigger automation workflows through Zapier
  5. AI analyzes reply intent and assigns a lead classification
  6. Qualified leads are routed for follow-up engagement

Key Outcomes:

  • Automated reply classification using AI
  • Reduced manual lead triaging
  • Structured follow-up process for qualified prospects
  • Scalable outbound lead engagement system

Tools Used: Apollo.io • Lemlist • Zapier • AI Reply Classification
