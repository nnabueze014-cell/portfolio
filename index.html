Project 1 – Lead Automation

Project Goal: Automate lead collection, follow-up, and team notifications to reduce manual effort and ensure timely responses to incoming leads.

How it was achieved: The automation was built using a no-code workflow on Zapier:

  1. Deployed a Google Form to collect leads from users.
  2. Form responses are automatically stored in Google Sheets(DATABASE).
  3. Google Sheets is used as the trigger in the Zapier workflow.
  4. When a new response is received, an automatic email is sent via Gmail to the responder to appreciate their submission.
  5. A task is created in HubSpot for the incoming lead.
  6. A follow-up task is automatically created on Asana to ensure timely follow-ups.
  7. A notification is sent to the designated Slack channel to inform the team responsible for the follow-up.

Tools used: Google Forms, Google Sheets, Zapier, Gmail, HubSpot CRM, Slack.
