Project 2 – Front-Desk to Support Operations with Automated SLA Escalations

Project Context:
This project was inspired by my hands-on learning journey in service desk operations, workflow automation, and project management tools. The goal was to design a realistic end-to-end support system that mirrors how real technical service companies handle customer requests and internal task execution.

Problem Statement:
Customer requests often arrive through unstructured channels such as phone calls or messages, resulting in delayed responses, missed follow-ups, and limited accountability.

System Design Overview:

  1. Customer Support Layer
    Customers submit requests through a support portal which creates structured tickets and automatically assigns priorities and SLAs.
  2. Automation Layer
    Event-driven workflows process ticket data and generate operational tasks automatically.
  3. Operations Layer
    Operational tasks are executed within a project management system where engineers track progress and SLA deadlines.

Key Outcomes:

  • End-to-end visibility from request to resolution
  • Automated handoff between support and operations
  • Clear SLA monitoring and escalation workflows
  • Improved accountability and response management

Concepts Applied: Service Desk Operations • SLA Monitoring • Automation Workflows • Escalation Logic • Cross-team Visibility

Project 3 – AI Lead Qualification & Cold Outreach Automation

Project Context:
Modern sales teams rely heavily on outbound prospecting, but manually reviewing replies and determining which leads are worth pursuing can slow down the sales pipeline.

Project Goal:
Design a system that automatically qualifies inbound email replies using AI and routes promising prospects into structured follow-up workflows.

System Workflow:

  1. Prospects are sourced using a lead intelligence platform
  2. Leads are verified and imported into an outbound email campaign
  3. Prospects receive a structured cold email sequence
  4. Email replies trigger automation workflows
  5. AI analyzes reply intent and assigns a lead score
  6. Qualified leads are automatically routed into follow-up workflows

Key Outcomes:

  • Automated lead qualification using AI reply analysis
  • Reduced manual lead triaging
  • Structured follow-up workflows for sales engagement
  • Scalable outbound lead generation pipeline

Tools Used: Apollo • Lemlist • Zapier • AI Reply Classification
